How the math works (click to expand)

The LP earns from five revenue streams. The simulator computes each stream from the inputs above and sums them to get the projected APY.

1. Base staking yield

The LP's investment is split into a stake portion (96.4%) and a dev funding portion (3.6%). The stake portion buys XDC, which is delegated to validators and earns the network reward (9% gross), minus the 15% protocol fee.

R₁ = I_stake × r × (1 − f)

where I_stake = LP investment net of dev contribution, r = 9% network APR, f = 15% protocol fee.

2. Validator operations margin

Each LP-funded node has its own operating P&L: 50% of the protocol fee it generates, minus per-node opex (~$2,250/year). The LP captures their pro-rata share of the cohort's validator margin.

R₂ = N_LP × (A_node × r × f × P × ν − C_opex,node)

where N_LP = LP's pro-rata cohort nodes, A_node = 10M XDC, ν = 50% (node ops share of fee), P = XDC price.

3. Protocol fee share

The protocol's share of the 15% fee accrues across the entire LST TVL (not just LP-funded stake). After protocol opex, the LP captures their tier rate × pro-rata of the resulting margin.

R₃ = ρ × λ × max(0, S × r × f × P × (1−ν) − C_opex,protocol)

where ρ = tier rate (0.20 / 0.30 / 0.40), λ = LP pro-rata in cohort = I/T, S = total LST TVL.

4. Outcome-based compensation (OBC)

A network-funded XDC bonus pool tied to active validator count. Pays monthly. Rate steps up at 20-node and 75-node milestones. The LP captures their tier rate × pro-rata of the pool.

R₄ = ρ × λ × N × A_node × ω(N) × 12 × P

where ω(N) = 0.0005 if N ≤ 20, 0.0006 if 20 < N ≤ 75, 0.0007 if N > 75.

How the tier rate works

Each LP's effective claim on the discretionary buckets (R₃, R₄) is ρ × λ, where ρ depends on ticket size. The remainder of the bucket stays with the protocol/foundation. Total LP cohort take depends on the cohort composition:

TierTicketRate (ρ)Per-$1k yield rate
Standard$250k20%1.0× baseline
Strategic$500k30%1.5× baseline
Anchor$1M40%2.0× baseline

Maximum cohort distribution = 40% of pool (when cohort is fully filled with anchors). With a mixed cohort the distributed share is lower. Example: 2×$250k + $500k + $1M cohort distributes 32.5% of the pool; the remaining 67.5% stays with the protocol/foundation.

5. Token mark-to-market (informational, not in APY)

If XDC price appreciates over the lock period, the LP captures 100% of the gain on their staked principal. The simulator assumes constant price, so this is not added to APY but shown separately when relevant.

Total

APY = (R₁ + R₂ + R₃ + R₄) / I

Smaller tickets land lower because the tier rate is smaller; the tradeoff is that the protocol/foundation retains a larger share of each bucket.
